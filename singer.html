A Simple Great Country Singer

George Strait is a great country singer. His simplicity in style enhances his great voice.
His songs are simple and full of meaning. He sings simple country songs. He does not mix country music with any other genre.
Some country artists have criticized the simplicity of his concerts and stage show, but they have not convinced him to change.
His fans do not see a need for him to change perfection. They love Strait just the way he is.
