National Natural Landmark

The State Park System's Only Geological Site

A sink hole that is 120 feet deep and 500 feet wide. The sink hole has a miniature rain forest. Small streams and fountains are just two of many features of the sink hole. Natural vegetation, shells, shark teeth and fossils remains line the walls of sinkhole for the casual naturalist in all of us.
